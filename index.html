About Mokobara

India's premium D2C travel brand

It makes polycarbonate hardshell luggage, backpacks, and travel accessories — combining European shell materials (German Makrolon) with Japanese hardware (Hinomoto wheels, YKK zippers) and selling directly to consumers without retail markups.

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before you buy

What material is the Mokobara Check-in Large made from?
The Mokobara Check-in Large uses a shell made from German Makrolon® polycarbonate — the same material used in aircraft cockpit windows and bulletproof glass. It is flexible enough to absorb impact without cracking, and lightweight enough to keep the bag's empty weight at 5.25 kg. Makrolon is manufactured by Covestro, a German specialty chemicals company.
How big is the Mokobara Check-in Large? Will it fit two weeks of clothes?
The Check-in Large has a 110-litre capacity and measures 51 × 31 × 79 cm. It includes a built-in compression system with linear straps that presses clothes flat to create extra space. Mokobara says it comfortably holds two weeks of clothing. It is accepted as check-in baggage by all major airlines — it is not cabin-approved.
What kind of wheels does the Mokobara Check-in Large have?
The suitcase has 8 Japanese Hinomoto spinner wheels — the same wheels used on premium European luggage brands like Rimowa and Samsonite's top-tier lines. They are double-wheeled, 360-degree spinners designed for near-silent movement on airport floors, marble, and carpet.
Does the Mokobara Check-in Large have a TSA lock?
Yes. The Check-in Large has a built-in TSA-approved number lock. It is keyless — you set your own combination code. TSA agents in the United States can open it with a master key without cutting the lock, which is a requirement for US-bound international travel.
What warranty does Mokobara offer on the Check-in Large?
Mokobara offers a 6-year warranty on the Check-in Large. This covers manufacturing defects and functional failures — broken wheels, handles, zippers, and hinges. If something fails, Mokobara will repair or replace the item. The brand is known for honouring this in full: multiple verified reviews report complete replacements, not just repairs.
Can I return the Mokobara Check-in Large if I don't like it?
Yes. Mokobara offers a 30-day risk-free return on all luggage. If you are not satisfied for any reason within 30 days of purchase, email support@mokobara.com to initiate a full refund. No conditions attached.
How does the Mokobara Check-in Large compare to Samsonite at the same price?
Mokobara uses the same grade of German Makrolon polycarbonate and Japanese Hinomoto wheels as international premium brands. At ~₹17,000, it competes directly with mid-tier Samsonite models but includes extras like a magic eraser, dust cover, laundry bag, and a 6-year warranty — which most Samsonite models at this price do not. The main trade-off: Samsonite has more international service centres for in-person repairs.
What accessories are included with the Mokobara Check-in Large?
Every Check-in Large ships with: a magic eraser (removes scuff marks from the shell), a dust cover (for storage between trips), a laundry bag (to separate dirty clothes during travel), and a compression pad with linear straps for more efficient packing.
